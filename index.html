About us

Our culinary vision focuses on delivering an authentic dining experience, showcasing Sri Lankan culture and flavors. We source seasonal ingredients from local farmers and fishermen, ensuring freshness and quality in every dish. Our restaurant supports Sri Lankan farming communities, blending colonial charm with contemporary design. The interior features a colonial charm and contemporary design, with a meticulously crafted mural wall showcasing cultural influences. Enjoy homemade dishes and relax.

This journey has been a labor of love, and we are deeply grateful for your support in bringing our vision to life. Your presence here is an integral part of our story, and we are honored to share this experience with you.
